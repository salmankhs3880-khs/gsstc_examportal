Examination Instructions

Please read all instructions carefully before starting the exam

  1. Time Limit: You have exactly 30 minutes to complete this examination. The timer will start immediately when you begin the test.
  2. Single Attempt: Each student is allowed only one attempt at this examination. Once you start, you cannot restart or retake the exam.
  3. Auto-Submission: The examination will automatically submit when:
    • The 30-minute timer expires
    • You click the submit button
  4. Browser Restrictions:
    • Do not refresh the page during the examination
    • Do not close the browser tab or window
    • Do not use the browser's back button
    • Do not open other tabs or applications
  5. Technical Issues: If you experience technical difficulties:
    • Note the exact time the issue occurred
    • Contact technical support immediately
    • Do not attempt to restart the exam on your own
  6. Academic Integrity:
    • This is an individual examination
    • No external help or resources are permitted
    • All answers must be your own work
  7. Results: Examination results will be available on the school portal 48 hours after the examination period ends.
